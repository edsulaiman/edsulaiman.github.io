Moto1 Customer
Mobile - Flutter

Moto1 Customer is a cutting-edge mobile application designed to revolutionize your motorcycle service and ownership experience. This customer-centric app is tailored exclusively for Moto1 workshop patrons, providing a seamless and personalized way to engage with the workshop and access a range of convenient services.

Key Features:

  1. Effortless Service Booking: Booking motorcycle services has never been easier. With Moto1 Customer, you can quickly schedule appointments for routine maintenance, repairs, or electric motorcycle conversions at the touch of a button.

  2. Real-Time Order Tracking: Stay informed about your motorcycle’s service progress in real-time. Track the status of your service order, from the moment it enters the workshop to completion, ensuring transparency and peace of mind.

  3. Personalized Notifications: Receive personalized notifications and updates regarding your motorcycle’s service status, exclusive offers, and important announcements. Never miss a beat with timely reminders for upcoming maintenance or special promotions.

  4. Digital Service Records: Access your motorcycle’s service history and digital service records conveniently within the app. Keep track of past maintenance and stay on top of recommended service intervals to ensure optimal performance.

  5. Easy Invoice Access and Payment: View and manage your service invoices digitally. Simplify payment with various secure and convenient options, including credit cards, digital wallets, and more, right from within the app.

  6. Exclusive Offers and Loyalty Rewards: Enjoy access to exclusive promotions, loyalty rewards, and discounts as a Moto1 Customer app user. Save on services, parts, and accessories, making your motorcycle ownership experience even more rewarding.

  7. Direct Communication with Workshop: Connect with the Moto1 workshop team directly through the app. Ask questions, provide feedback, and receive prompt responses from the workshop staff to address your concerns.

  8. User-Friendly Interface: Moto1 Customer app boasts a user-friendly interface, making it simple and enjoyable to navigate through its features. Whether you’re a tech-savvy rider or new to mobile apps, you’ll find it intuitive and easy to use.

Moto1 Customer puts the power of exceptional motorcycle service and ownership in the palm of your hand. Say goodbye to the hassles of traditional service booking and hello to a seamless, personalized experience with Moto1 Customer app. Elevate your motorcycle journey today!
