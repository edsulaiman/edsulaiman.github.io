Moto1 Back Office
Mobile - Flutter

Moto1 Back Office is a comprehensive Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) application specially designed for the Moto1 workshop, empowering efficient management of employee, sales, marketing, finance, accounting, and inventory processes. This powerful app centralizes all aspects of workshop operations, ensuring seamless coordination and optimizing productivity.

Key Features:

  1. Employee Management: Efficiently manage your workforce with Moto1 Back Office. From HR-related tasks such as attendance tracking, leave management, and performance evaluation to task assignments and scheduling, this app streamlines employee-related processes for increased efficiency.

  2. Sales and Marketing: Maximize sales and marketing efforts with the app’s integrated tools. Track leads, manage customer interactions, and analyze sales performance to make informed decisions and enhance customer engagement.

  3. Finance and Accounting: Simplify financial management with Moto1 Back Office. Track income, expenses, and profits, generate financial reports, and manage invoicing and billing processes effortlessly. Stay on top of your workshop’s financial health with real-time insights.

  4. Inventory Control: Optimize inventory management with the app’s advanced features. Track stock levels, manage supplier relationships, and automate reordering to ensure seamless workshop operations and prevent stockouts.

  5. Customer Relationship Management (CRM): Build strong customer relationships using the integrated CRM module. Access customer profiles, service histories, and communication logs to offer personalized service and strengthen customer loyalty.

  6. Performance Analytics: Leverage data-driven insights with the app’s robust analytics tools. Monitor workshop performance, analyze key metrics, and identify growth opportunities to drive continuous improvement.

  7. Internal Communication and Collaboration: Facilitate seamless communication and collaboration among workshop staff with in-app messaging and collaboration features. Keep everyone informed and aligned for efficient teamwork.

  8. Data Security and Compliance: Moto1 Back Office ensures the security and confidentiality of your workshop’s data. Robust data protection measures keep sensitive information safe, ensuring compliance with data privacy regulations.

  9. Customizable Dashboard and Reports: Tailor the app’s dashboard and reports to suit your workshop’s specific needs. Visualize critical data at a glance and generate custom reports to gain deeper insights into your workshop’s performance.

Moto1 Back Office serves as the ultimate ERP solution, uniting employee management, sales, marketing, finance, accounting, and inventory control in a single, user-friendly platform. By streamlining operations and providing real-time insights, this app empowers Moto1 workshop managers to make informed decisions and deliver exceptional service to their customers.
