Moto1 Pos
Mobile - Flutter

Moto1 POS is a dedicated Point of Sale (POS) application designed exclusively for the Moto1 workshop, streamlining the order management process with speed and precision. This user-friendly app is tailored to efficiently handle all your workshop’s order transactions, from quick service tasks to converting motorcycles to electric vehicles.

Key Features:

  1. Efficient Order Reception: Moto1 POS simplifies the order reception process, allowing your workshop staff to swiftly capture customer requests. With a clean and intuitive interface, taking orders becomes seamless, minimizing wait times and enhancing customer satisfaction.

  2. Quick Service Orders: For routine maintenance and quick service tasks, Moto1 POS enables you to process orders efficiently. Assign jobs to technicians, track progress in real-time, and keep customers informed about their motorcycle’s status throughout the service.

  3. Electric Motorcycle Conversion Orders: Embrace the future of sustainable transportation with Moto1 POS’s specialized module for electric motorcycle conversions. Receive and manage conversion orders with ease, ensuring a smooth workflow for these innovative projects.

  4. Customer Information Management: Moto1 POS features a built-in customer database, providing easy access to your customers’ details and order history. Keep track of contact information, preferred services, and previous orders, empowering you to offer personalized recommendations and exceptional service.

  5. Real-Time Order Tracking: Stay on top of all your workshop orders with real-time order status tracking. From the moment an order is received until it is completed, monitor progress and manage resources effectively to meet customer expectations.

  6. Seamless Digital Invoicing: Generate professional invoices directly from the app. Provide transparent pricing to your customers and streamline the payment process for a hassle-free transaction experience.

  7. Multiple Payment Options: Moto1 POS supports various payment methods, making it convenient for customers to settle their bills. Accept cash, card payments, or digital wallets effortlessly.

  8. Customizable User Permissions: Maintain control over the app’s access and functionality by assigning customizable user permissions. Restrict access to sensitive data and ensure that each staff member can only perform authorized actions.

Moto1 POS is tailored to meet the unique needs of your motorcycle workshop, focusing solely on the Point of Sale operations. By optimizing the order management process, this app ensures that your workshop operates efficiently and provides exceptional service to your valued customers.
